Earth Sciences

The way Earth Sciences is taught is special within the physical sciences, as the central dogma is built up from observations instead of theoretical axioms (e.g. in math or physics). In practice, this means that the demand for bottom-up logic isn't as high as say in math or physical chemistry, but gathering intuition about geological observations from familiarizing yourself with the subject over time is very important.
